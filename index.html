Nigerian Stew

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves : 5
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 6
Onions 2
Bell Peppers 3
Habanero 3
Vegetable Oil 10 fl OZ
Choice of Seasoning N/A

Step 1: Wash and chop tomatoes, bell peppers and one onion in big chunks. Remove stock of pepper only. Chop the other onion in medium chunks or slices.

Step 2:Throw all in a blender except the second onions, blend all depending on what king of texture you prefer (rough or smooth).

Step 3:When done, pour your puree into a big enough pot and place on a burner and heat. Check regularly to prevent it from spilling over the pot,do not cover the pot; the goal is to remove as much water as you can.

Step 4:Once dry enough heat up the oil in another pot at least the same size or a little smaller.

Step 5:Gently pour the onions into the oil when hot. Let cook for about 30 seconds to a minute then pour your puree

Step 6:Let fry in the oil for a couple minutes. Season, taste then continue to let it cook.Stir regularly.

Step 7:When done serve with the food of your choice (rice, pasta, plantain, etc).

Nigerian Jollof Rice

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves : 5
Ingredients Quantity
Nigerian Stew 40 fl oz
Onions 1
Choice of Seasoning N/A
Tomatoes 2
Parboiled Rice 2 1/2 cups

Step 1: Wash rice and let boil for 10 minutes.

Step 2:Take rice off the fire and pour into a sieve to drain. Run cold water over it to stop the cooking

Step 3:Take the rice back into the pot and onto the fire. Pour your already made stew into the pot of rice and add water as well. Taste and season as necessary

Step 4:Cover and let cook. Try not to open too many times

Step 5:Toss in chopped onions and tomatoes when almost dry and gently mix in

Step 6:Ready to serve

Chicken Fried Rice

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves : 7
Ingredients Quantity
Oyster Sauce ~25 fl oz
Onions 2
Soy Sauce 1/2 cup
Green Onions 4
Jasmine Rice 3 1/2 cups
Chicken 2 lbs
Large Eggs 5
Vegetable Oil 2 1/2 cups

Step 1:Boil rice as you would normally

Step 2:Slice chicken into small strips and stir fry. Season the way you want.

Step 3:Slice green onions into small pieces.

Step 4:Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat.

Step 5:In a pot, add 2 1/2cups of oil. When hot gently pour the eggs in while stiring simultaneously.The rice should be done at this point

Step 6:Next pour the rice into the same pot as the eggs and stir thoroughly.

Step 7:Add 1/2 a cup of soy sauce and then oyster sauce to your taste and mix thoroughly.

Step 7:Toss in the chicken and green onions once you are about to switch off the heat and stir

Step 7:Ready to serve
